How We Work

A Process Built
for Clarity

01

Brief & Discovery

We begin by deeply understanding your project — its architecture, mood, target audience, and vision. A detailed brief ensures every render is purposeful.

02

Modelling & Composition

We build or receive the 3D model, then carefully compose camera angles, lighting, and environmental context to maximize visual storytelling.

03

Draft & Feedback

You receive a grey-shaded draft for composition approval before we apply materials, lighting, and post-production — saving time and ensuring alignment.

04

Final Delivery

High-resolution, print-ready renders are delivered in your preferred format with up to two rounds of revisions included in every project.
